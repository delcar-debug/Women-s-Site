The Workout

🎄 Every year around Christmas time, we do the "12 Days of Christmas" Workout — a fun team tradition to celebrate the season.

It follows the same building pattern as the song, except instead of a partridge in a pear tree, each number gets its own exercise. On "1," you just do exercise 1. On "2," you do exercise 2, then circle back for exercise 1. By "12," you're moving through all twelve exercises in order and then back down to 1 — a new "gift" added at every number, just like the song.

Wrestlers race the clock through the whole ladder, and their time becomes their score — and maybe they'll make the leaderboard below. 🏆

1Run to the drinking fountain at the far end of the room and back
2Dummy Headlocks
3Back pedal (3 mat lengths)
4Box jumps
5Overhead bag circles (5 each side for 10 total)
6Sprawls and circle up
7Sit Stands
8Burpees (Chest to ground and jump at the top)
9Russian Twists (9 each direction for 18 total)
10Bag Slams
11Frog Jumps (as far as you can)
12Rope Pulls (1 full loop = one rep)

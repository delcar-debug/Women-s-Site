New Wrestler FAQ

Everything you need to know before joining the team — no experience required.

Why should I join wrestling?

  • Mostly because it’s fun! The number one reason our wrestlers continue to wrestle is because they have fun. We go sledding, have team dinners, dress up for Christmas, have our own photo shoot, play games in practice, travel to overnight meets, and more.
  • You can also find your strength by working through challenges, create new friendships, develop confidence, and increase athleticism for other sports.

Do I have to wrestle boys?

  • Never. Girls get to wrestle only girls in practices and competitions.
  • This is the same for our youth and middle school levels. Girls never have to wrestle boys.
  • Did you know that Women’s Wrestling is the fastest growing sport in the country?!

Would I have to lose weight?

  • No, there are no requirements to lose weight to be at a certain weight class.
  • You pick what weight class you would like to be.

Would I have to wear a singlet?

  • No. A singlet is not required. Other uniforms are available.

Is high school too late to join wrestling?

  • Definitely not! Almost all of our high school wrestlers did not join until high school, including Aini Anderson, our state finalist, and other state placewinners.

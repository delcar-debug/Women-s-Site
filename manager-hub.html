Expectations

General Expectations

  1. Complete all the tasks on the Manager's List Spreadsheet.
  2. Although a lot of the expectations in the team handbook apply only to wrestlers, managers still need to follow the expectations on culture, mindset, letting Coach Carl know when they will be gone, being on time, and any other expectation that is not wrestling specific.
  3. There is no requirement to attend practices. Just be around enough to complete the list.
    • There are daily and weekly tasks that can be rotated between managers.
  4. Attend every meet or arrange things with Carl in advance of missing a meet.
  5. Take Matboss video during meets.

Thank you for being a part of our program! We appreciate everything you do for us!

Task Details — Pre Meet

  1. Bring iPads
  2. Restock/Organize Medkit
    • Ask other managers or compare contents to the boys kit. Also, make sure there are tampons, pads (various sizes of both), hair ties, hair rubber bands, tape, re-wrap, and band aids.
    • Make sure finals singlets are in the bag
  3. Bring Medkit

Task Details — Post Meet

  1. Upload Matboss videos
    • Plug in the iPads prior to leaving for home after the meet and go to "Upload"
  2. Matboss Verify
    • Compare to USA Bracketing and see what matches are missing. Correct scores on Matboss if needed also.
